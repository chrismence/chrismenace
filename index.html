I don't remember why I took these?

Hello, My name is Chris Mence and, this website is the self-proposed task to learn about processing by developing this website. The art direction for this project. It was intentional to learn HTML while being interested in creating moving content for this site and recommend viewing on the desktop browser.

I was interested in a relationship with media ecology concerning social and cultural image-making: as you view the fragmented gallery of animated gifs that may resemble as experiences or memories dependable on interpretation. My inspirations drawled by questioning what photography is today in the age of social media; as an accumulation of images is so rapid by trying to caption our sense of place though by producing visual messages.

Each photograph is read as the private appearance of its referent: the age of Photography corresponds precisely to the explosion of the private into the public, or rather into the creation of a new social value, which is the publicity of the private: the private is consumes as such, publicly.” ―Roland Barthes, Camera Lucida: Reflections on Photography
